Overview — System Integration

Welcome to System Integration! This week, you'll bring together all the skills you've learned—mechanics, electronics, programming, and interfaces—into a complete, working system. This is where your final project comes together.

We'll focus on mapping all subsystems, planning integration steps, identifying risks, and testing the complete system. By the end of the week, you'll have a working prototype that demonstrates how all components work together to solve a real problem in your community.

Learning Objectives

  • Map all subsystems of your project (power, sensors, outputs, structure, enclosure, interface).
  • Plan integration steps and identify risks or dependencies.
  • Test the full system and iterate on weak points.

Assignments

  • Individual: Produce a system diagram for your final project and show that all components are connected and working together.
  • Group: Conduct informal “integration reviews” where peers help you find missing pieces and potential failures.

Documentation & Evidence

  • Block diagrams or system maps of the full project.
  • Photos/videos of the complete system running through its main use case.
  • Checklist of open issues and ideas for future improvements beyond the course.
