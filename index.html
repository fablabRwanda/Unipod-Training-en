Empowering Africa's Future with UNIPOD Digital Fabrication Program

In partnership with Timbuktoo, UNDP, FabLab Rwanda and UNIPODS, we're delivering cutting-edge technology education across Africa. Join our mission to build digital skills for the next generation.

Lab & Equipment Training Focus

Design Lab

A creative space for turning ideas into digital models using CAD, graphics, and simulation tools — the starting point before any physical build.
Rapid Prototyping Lab

Equipped with 3D printers, laser cutters, and other tools to quickly transform digital designs into physical prototypes for testing and iteration.
Mechanical Lab

Focused on machining, metalwork, and mechanical systems — where students learn how to engineer moving parts and durable structures.
Woodworking Lab

A hands-on workshop for crafting with wood using both traditional tools and digital fabrication methods, blending craftsmanship with technology.
Electrical & Electronic Lab

A hub for building, testing, and programming circuits, sensors, and devices — powering ideas through electronics and embedded systems.
Textile Lab

Dedicated to fabric innovation, combining sewing, embroidery, and digital textile tools to explore wearable tech, smart fabrics, and design.
Virtual Reality Lab

An immersive lab for exploring VR/AR technologies, digital simulations, and interactive 3D experiences that extend learning beyond physical limits.
Green Tech Lab

A sustainability-focused lab where renewable energy, eco-friendly materials, and clean technologies are tested to address environmental challenges.
Food & Agritech Lab

An innovation space for experimenting with agricultural technologies, food processing, and smart farming solutions to strengthen food security.

Your 12-Month Roadmap to Mastery

1

Foundation (Months 1-2)

Build your maker mindset. Master design thinking principles and CAD fundamentals. Learn to see problems as opportunities for creative solutions.

Introduction
Version Control
Design Thinking
Computer-Aided Design
2

Rapid Prototyping (Months 3-4)

Bring digital designs into the physical world. Master 3D printing, laser cutting, and 3D scanning to iterate quickly and effectively.

3D Scanning and Printing
Computer-Controlled Cutting
3

Electronics (Months 5-6)

Add intelligence to your creations. Dive into circuits, microcontrollers, and sensors to build responsive, interactive prototypes.

Electronics Design
Electronics Production
Embedded Programming
Input Devices
Output Devices
4

Mechanical & Fabrication (Months 7-8)

Level up with heavy-duty tools. Learn CNC machining, welding, and advanced fabrication techniques for robust, professional-grade builds.

Computer-Controlled Machining
Molding and Casting
5

Integration & Capstone (Months 9-12)

Synthesize everything you've learned. Design and build your masterpiece project that showcases your complete skill set and creative vision.

Networking and Communications
Interface and Application Programming
System Integration
Invention, Intellectual Property, and Income
Final Project Presentations
6

Textile, VR , GreenTech, Food & AgriTech (Months 9-11)

Diversify your maker skills. Combine electronics with fabric to create interactive wearables, build virtual prototypes in VR, and tackle real-world challenges in renewable energy, recycling, and food production technology.
